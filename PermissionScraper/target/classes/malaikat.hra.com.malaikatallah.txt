We pride ourselves on offering entertaining Apps for family audience 
of all ages. Because we want your child to use 
our Apps in a safe environment, we have limited the 
collection and usage of the collected Information for Apps that 
are directed to children. We have also integrated an age 
gate in order to limit the collection and usage of 
Information for users under the age of 13 in specific 
territories (US included) for the mixed audience Apps. The age 
gate is being conducted after the download of some of 
our Apps for specific territories in order to assure the 
privacy and protection of younger users of our apps. If
